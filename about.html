Set in Dublin City in 2019, this contemporary take on William Shakespeare’s Romeo & Juliet is a tale of domestic abuse and a young girl controlled by the two most pivotal male figures in her life, Romeo and her Father. Set in an urban venue with a soundtrack of Dublin’s finest hiphop and stunning visuals from street art collective, Subset, this performance is set to shock you and leave you seething until a triumphant ending which will make you stand to cheer and applaud. Utterly heartbreaking and thought provoking, this play aims to highlight issues involving underage relationships, domestic abuse and control.
