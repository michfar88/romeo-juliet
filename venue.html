Tucked away on the cobblestoned streets of Dublin’s Temple Bar, Meeting House is an urban outdoor space. Once home to a nightclub and bar but left derelict for many years, we are now taking over to use it as a creative performance space. Street art and grafitti has been created by collective, Subset, and music on the night will be provided by some of Dublin’s most talented hiphop artists.

Access: All Dublin Bus, Luas and DART routes to Dublin City Centre. Main access to Sycamore Street from Dame Street or East Essex Street, Dublin 2.

Dress: Dress for all seasons! This is an outdoor event but we will provide you with cushions, blankets and hot water bottles for your comfort.

Refreshments from 7pm, show starts at 8pm followed by hiphop performances.
