Nick Kelly is a household name in Ireland. He has starred in some of the best Irish movies in the last ten years. Director, Michelle Farley, has been a fan of him for many years and finally found a gap in both their schedules to work alongside one another in Romeo & Juliet.
Fran Carey has been acting since the age of twelve and starred in countless productions and plays throughout Ireland and the UK. Winner of some prestigious awards she has just signed a contract to star in her first feature length film with legend, Cillian Murphy.

Brendan Gannon is known for playing dark characters and there could be no one more suited for the role of “Father”. Having worked alongside Kelly previously, they have excellent chemistry on stage which is only amplified by a stunning performance by Fran Carey.
This is renowned director, Michelle Farley’s, sixth Shakespeare adaptation in Dublin City. Always bringing to light pressing issues and taboo subjects all while educating and helping to protect those in need. A feminist and strong supporter of domestic abuse charities.
